Private by design

Not a promise. An architecture.

No server to hack

Your data isn't “encrypted in the cloud” — it's simply not in any cloud. There is no Money OS database of users, because there are no users to store. A breach of us leaks nothing about you.

Verifiable in 10 seconds

Open the app, press F12, choose Network, use it. The only requests you'll see are public price data — a shared price file identical for every user, with nothing about you in it. Your numbers never appear in any request.

Works on a plane

Airplane mode is the ultimate privacy test, and Money OS passes it. Budget in a cabin, on a flight, in a blackout — the file doesn't care.

The honest trade

Apps with bank sync are convenient — and they read your transactions on their servers. We made the opposite trade: you paste a CSV once a month, and in return nobody, including us, ever sees a single number. You decide which trade is right for you.

How do I receive it?

Instantly by email: a single file that opens in any browser on any device — Mac, Windows, iPhone, Android. No installation, no account.

Can you prove it's private?

Yes — technically. Open the app, press F12 → Network tab and use it: the only traffic you'll see is live prices. The app downloads public price data — a shared price file that is identical for every user and contains nothing about you, plus a symbol-only lookup for less-common coins. Your amounts, your names, your goals — none of it ever appears in any request. Either way, your numbers have no server to go to, and if we disappeared tomorrow your file keeps working.

Is my financial data safe?

Safer than any app with a server: your data lives only in your own browser. Use the built-in Backup button to keep a copy anywhere you like.
